# Business Case

## The Problem

SMB operations teams are drowning in fragmented data across spreadsheets, PDFs, and siloed tools. They lack the resources for dedicated analysts, leading to:

- **Lost institutional knowledge** when employees leave
- **Reactive decision-making** without data-driven insights
- **Hours wasted** searching for information across systems
- **Missed optimization opportunities** in vendor spend and processes

## Our Solution

OpsOrchestra is an AI-powered Business Operations Analyst that:

1. **Ingests** documents from any format (PDF, CSV, Excel)
2. **Builds** a knowledge graph of entities and relationships
3. **Answers** questions with context-aware, cited responses
4. **Generates** executive reports and KPI tracking
5. **Learns** and improves with every interaction

## Market Opportunity

- **Target:** SMBs with 50-500 employees
- **Pain point:** $50K-$150K/year for a human analyst
- **Our positioning:** AI analyst at a fraction of the cost

## Differentiation

| Competitor | Gap |
|------------|-----|
| ChatGPT/Claude | No persistent memory, no document grounding |
| Notion AI | Limited to Notion ecosystem |
| Microsoft Copilot | Enterprise-focused, complex licensing |
| **OpsOrchestra** | Purpose-built for BizOps, knowledge graph, decision tracing |

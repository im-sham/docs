# Pitch Narrative

## The Vision

Every SMB deserves an AI analyst that remembers everything about their operations and gets smarter with every conversation.

## The Problem

Operations teams at SMBs (50-500 employees) struggle with:

- **Fragmented data** across spreadsheets, PDFs, and tools
- **No dedicated analyst** — can't afford $100K+ salary
- **Lost knowledge** when employees leave
- **Reactive mode** — fighting fires instead of optimizing

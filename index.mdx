# OpsOrchestra

> AI-powered Business Operations Analyst that builds institutional knowledge with every interaction.

## Overview

OpsOrchestra is an AI-first BizOps platform for SMB operations teams. It combines conversational AI with document intelligence, knowledge graphs, and automated reporting.

## Core Capabilities

- **Document Intelligence**: Ingest PDFs, CSVs, Excel with automatic entity extraction
- **Knowledge Graph**: Relationships between companies, people, metrics, tools
- **RAG-Enhanced Chat**: Context-aware responses grounded in your documents
- **Decision Tracing**: Full audit trail with OpenTelemetry-compatible IDs
- **Report Generation**: Executive summaries and KPI reports
